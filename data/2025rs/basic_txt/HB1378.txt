AN ACT concerning  1 
 
Civil Actions – Child Sexual Abuse Claims Against the State – Time Limitation  2 
 
FOR the purpose of prohibiting certain actions under the Child Victims Act against the 3 
State or a unit of State government from being filed on or after a certain date  altering 4 
certain provisions relating to the award of noneconomic damages to a claimant in an 5 
action for child sexual abuse; altering cer tain provisions relating to the liability of 6 
the State under the Maryland Tort Claims Act and of a local government under the 7 
Local Government Tort Claims Act for certain claims of child sexual abuse; altering 8 
certain provisions relating to the defense of sovereign immunity by a county board 9 
of education to certain claims of child sexual abuse; altering certain provisions 10 
relating to minimum liability insurance coverage requirements for county boards of 11 
education ; and generally relating to civil actions for  child sexual abuse.  12
