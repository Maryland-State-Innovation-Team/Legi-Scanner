FOR the purpose of requiring that certain applications for a license, an identification card, 5 
or a moped operator’s permit allow an applicant to choose to indicate on the 6 
document the applicant’s nonapparent disability; requiring the Motor Vehicle 7 
Administ ration to ensure that the license, identification card, or moped operator’s 8 
permit of an applicant who indicates that the applicant has a nonapparent disability 9 
includes a certain notation; requiring the Administration to establish certain public 10 
outreach efforts on the availability of the notation; requiring the Administration to 11 
delete a certain record about an applicant’s nonapparent disability notation under 12 
certain circumstances;  prohibiting the Administration from disclosing certain 13 
information to in a certain entities  manner under certain circumstances  about an 14 
applicant that indicates that the applicant has a nonapparent disability; and 15 
generally relating to nonapparent disability notations on licenses, identification 16 
cards, and moped operator’s perm its. 17
