FOR the purpose of defining “anchor date ” to establish certain reference points for the 4 
calculation of a certain covered employee’s average weekly wage and eligibility for 5 
increases in weekly benefits under the Family Medical Leave and Insurance 6 
Program;  requiring the Maryland Department of Labor  to adopt regulations 7 
establishing an optional self –employed enrollment program governing the 8 
participation of self –employed individuals in the Family and Medical Leave 9 
Insurance  Program; repealing certain requirements related to the payment of 10 
contributio ns to the Program by participating self –employed individuals; altering 11 
certain dates related to annual reporting and the implementation of the Program;  12 
excluding participating self –employed individuals from certain provisions of law 13 
governing the payment o f benefits under the Program; altering the definition of 14 
“application year” for purposes of the Program; altering the frequency with which 15 
subsequent payments are to be made under the Program; authorizing the Secretary 16 
to announce, rather than notify each employer of, a certain increase to the maximum 17 
weekly benefit amount; prohibiting the Department from requiring certain 18 
employers to escrow employer and employee contributions before the issuance of 19 
Program benefits by the Department;  and generally relatin g to the Family and 20 
Medical Leave Insurance Program.  21
