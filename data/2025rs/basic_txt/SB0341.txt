AN ACT concerning  1 
 
Baltimore City – Raffles – Organizations Affiliated With a Professional Major 2 
League Baseball Team  3 
 
FOR the purpose of authorizing certain organizations that are affiliated with a professional 4 
major league baseball team that plays its home games in Baltimore City to conduct 5 
certain raffles; requiring the organization to obtain a permit to conduct raffles  from 6 
an agency designated by the Baltimore City government; requiring that raffle tickets 7 
are sold using age –verification measures to ensure that the buyer is at least a certain 8 
age; authorizing a raffle ticket to be sold and received in–person or  on an electronic 9 
device located within the a certain  geographical boundaries of  area in  Baltimore City; 10 
providing that, subject to certain limitations, an organization conducting a raffle 11 
under this Act is not subject to a certain provision of law that pr ohibits the 12 
organization from accepting credit as payment;  and generally relating to raffles in 13 
Baltimore City.  14
