AN ACT concerning  1 
 
Election Law – Local Boards of Elections – Language –Related Assistance  2 
Language Assistance Program  3 
 
FOR the purpose of requiring the State Board of Elections to determine whether there is a 4 
significant and substantial need for language –related assistance in certain 5 
languages by a local board of elections during an election based on certain criteria; 6 
requ iring local boards to provide certain language –related assistance and materials 7 
in voting and elections to certain voters in certain languages; requiring local boards 8 
to make reasonable efforts to recruit election judges who are fluent in certain 9 
languages ; and generally relating to language –related assistance  establishing the 10 
Language Assistance Program; requiring a local board of elections to participate in 11 
the Program if the State Board determines that there is a significant need for 12 
language assistance to voters in the county in a language other than English; 13 
requiring the State Board to designate, not later than a certain date, certain 14 
languages other than English in which a local board that is required to participate 15 
in the Program must provide assista nce to voters; requiring a local board that is 16 
required to participate in the Program to provide, with oversight from the State 17 
Board, certain language –related services to voters; and generally relating to the 18 
Language Assistance Program . 19
