 (a) (1) In order to assure that an increasing population, accompanied by 19 
expanding settlement and growing mechanization, does not occu py and modify all areas 20 
within the State, leaving no lands designated for preservation and protection in their 21 
natural condition, it is the policy of the General Assembly to secure for the people of present 22 
and future generations the benefits of an endurin g resource of State wildlands.  23

enjoyment as wildlands, to provide for their protection, preservation of their wildland 29 
character, and for gathering and dissemination of inform ation regarding their use and 30 
enjoyment as wildlands.  31
