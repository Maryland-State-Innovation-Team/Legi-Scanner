AN ACT concerning  1 
 
Somerset County Code of Public Local Laws – 2025 Edition – Legalization  2 
 
FOR the purpose of legalizing the 20 25 Edition of the Somerset County Code of Public Local 3 
Laws and any supplement to the extent to which that code or supplement contains 4 
laws enacted by the General Assembly; and generally relating to the 20 25 Edition of 5 
the Somerset County Code of Public Loca l Laws.  6 
 
 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND, 7 
That the 20 25 Edition of the Somerset County Code of Public Local Laws and any 8 
supplement to the 20 25 Edition of the Somerset County Code of Public Local Laws, to the 9 
extent to which that code or supplement contains laws enacted by the General Assembly of 10 
Maryland, is legalized. The 20 25 Edition of the Somerset County Code of Public Local Laws 11 
and any sup plement shall be deemed and taken in all the courts of the State and by all 12 
public officials of the State and its political subdivisions to be evidence of the law enacted 13 
by the General Assembly contained in the code or a supplement to it.  14 
 
 SECTION 2. AND  BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July 15
