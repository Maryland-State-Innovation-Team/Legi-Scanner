Health Insurance – Provider Directory – Required Updates  2 
 
FOR the purpose of renaming “network directory” to be “provider directory” to conform to 3 
a certain federal law; altering the time period within which a carrier is  certain 4 
carriers are  required to update the information that must be made available in the 5 
carrier’s provider directory on the Internet; requiring a carrier to verify and, as 6 
necessary, update the carrier’s provider directory with a certain frequency  altering 7 
the information required to be included in a provider directory; requiring that a 8 
provider directory in printed form include a certain statement regarding the 9 
accuracy of the provider information ; and generally relating to provider directories 10 
of health insurance carriers .  11
