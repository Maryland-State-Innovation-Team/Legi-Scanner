State Retirement and Pension System – Administrative Fees – Repeal  2 
 
FOR the purpose of requiring the Board of Trustees for the State Retirement and Pension 3 
System to certify certain employer contribution rates each fiscal year; requiring the 4 
employer contribution rates to include recognition of certain administrative and 5 
operational expenses; repealing a requirement that certain administrative and 6 
operational expenses of the State Retirement and Pension System and the State 7 
Retirement Agency be funded by charging each participating employer an 8 
administrative fee for each sy stem participant; clarifying that certain payments 9 
certified by the Board of Trustees to be paid by local employers in a certain fiscal 10 
year shall be paid;  requiring the Board of Trustees to fund certain operating 11 
expenses of the State Retirement Agency th rough the accumulation fund; and 12 
generally relating to the funding of the administrative and operating expenses of the 13 
State Retirement and Pension System and the State Retirement Agency.  14
