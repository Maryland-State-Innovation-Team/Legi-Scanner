CHAPTER ______  
 
AN ACT concerning  1 
 
Family Law – Adoption of an Adult  2 
 
FOR the purpose of limiting the requirement that a petitioner’s spouse join in the petition 3 
for adoption to apply only when the prospective adoptee is a minor; authorizing a 4 
petitioner’s spouse to join in the petition for adoption if the prospective adoptee is an 5 
adult; establishing that certain provisions of law requiring the issuance and service 6 
of a show –cause order on a prospective adoptee’s parents do not apply if the 7 
prospective adoptee is an adult; authorizing a court to order, for good cause shown, 8 
certain investigati ons before ruling on certain adoption petitions involving a 9 
prospective adoptee who is an adult; establishing that parental consent to an 10 
adoption is not required if the prospective adoptee is an adult; and generally relating 11 
to the adoption of an adult.  12
