altering the rule of interpretation for “veteran”; altering the application of certain 4 
provisions of law governing natural resources to apply to all uniformed services, 5 
rather than only the armed forces; altering the circumstances under which licenses 6 
for fishing in tidal waters do not lapse; authorizing active service members, veterans, 7 
and vehicles of which an active service member or veteran is an occupant to enter a 8 
State  park without paying a certain fee; and generally relating to service members 9 
and veterans.  10
