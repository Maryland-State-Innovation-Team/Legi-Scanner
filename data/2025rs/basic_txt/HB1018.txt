 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That §§ 5 –514(b), 6 –606.1(b), and 9 
11–522(a) of the Financial Institutions Article, as enacted by Section 1 of this Act, shall be 10 
construed to apply retroactively and sh all be applied to and interpreted to affect any 11 
conventional home mortgage loan entered into prior to the effective date of this Act. Any 12 
conventional home mortgage that exceeds the lending limits set by the Federal Housing 13 
Finance Agency entered into befo re the effective date of this Act shall be deemed to include 14 
a provision allowing for any of the existing borrowers to purchase the property interest of 15 
another borrower on the loan by assuming the seller’s portion of the mortgage pursuant to 16 
a decree of a bsolute divorce entered on or after the effective date of this Act and  in accordance 17 
with Section 1 of this Act.  18
