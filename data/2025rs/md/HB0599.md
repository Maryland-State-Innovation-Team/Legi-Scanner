17 ASSOCIATION’S LABOR ARBITRATION PANEL.

18 2. THE PARTIES SHALL SELECT THE ARBITRATOR BY
19 ALTERNATELY STRIKING A NAME FROM THE LIST UNTIL ONE NAME REMAINS.

20 3. IF THE SELECTED ARBITRATOR IS UNABLE TO

22 SHALL BE REPEATED USING A NEW LIST OF ARBITRATORS UNTIL AN ARBITRATOR IS
23 SELECTED.

24 4. THE PARTIES SHALL SUBMIT THE DISPUTE AND THE
25 LAST, BEST, AND FINAL OFFERS TO THE ARBITRATOR WITHIN 5 BUSINESS DAYS
26 AFTER THE ARBITRATOR IS SELECTED.
